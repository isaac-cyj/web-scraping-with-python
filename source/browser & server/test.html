IgnITE Skills Challenge 2021

Organised by the Institute of Technical Education (ITE), the IgnITE Skills Challenge is an annual national competition that is dedicated to Normal Stream students and challenges them to showcase their technical prowess in a range of skills areas.

Find out more

Calling Jobseekers

We welcome Singapore citizens and permanent residents, who are seeking jobs. Starting from July 2020, you can pick up skills from ITE's certifiable short courses while receiving a monthly allowance.

Find out more

Two Free CET Courses for ITE Graduates

All Full-Time and Traineeship Nitec graduates in Dec 2019, Higher Nitec graduates in Mar 2020 and ITE graduates who have completed their National Service between Jan 2020 to Jun 2020 can enjoy up to two free CET courses (Certificate of Competency, and Modules for Skills Training for Nitec/Higher Nitec)!

Find out more

Elevated Safe-distancing Measures in Light of COVID-19 Situation

ITE will continue alternating between Home-Based Learning (HBL) and On-Campus Training on a weekly basis. The new training schedule can be found at https://www.ite.edu.sg/admissions/academic-calendar. For Continuing Education and Training (CET) students, on-campus training have resumed. For more details, visit our Elevated Safe-distancing Measures in Light of COVID-19 Situation page.

Find out more

Nominate your favourite lecturer or trainer for an award

We welcome ITE staff, students and parents, CET participants and employers, and industry partners to nominate inspiring ITE lecturers and CET trainers! Nominations are open from 8 Feb to 19 Mar 2021, 5pm.
